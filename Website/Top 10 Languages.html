List of languages by native of speakers

The following table contains the top 10 languages by estimated number of native speakers in the 2007 edition of Nationalencyklopedin. As census methods in different countries vary to a considerable extent, and given that some countries do not record language in their censuses, any list of languages by native speakers, or total speakers, is effectively based on estimates.

Rank Language Native speakers in millions Fraction of world population
1 Mandarin (entire branch) 935 14.1%
2 Spanish 390 5.85%
3 English 365 5.52%
4 Hindi 295 4.46%
5 Arabic 280 4.23%
6 Portuguese 205 3.08%
7 Bengali 200 3.05%
8 Russian 160 2.42%
9 Japanese 125 1.92%
10 Punjabi 95 1.44%
